# 🛒 E-commerce Customer & Revenue Analytics

##  Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing an e-commerce platform's customer behavior, revenue performance, and operational insights using data analytics techniques.

The objective is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights by performing:

- Data exploration and quality analysis
- Data cleaning and transformation
- Feature engineering
- Customer segmentation using RFM analysis
- Revenue and customer behavior analysis
- Interactive dashboard creation using Power BI


---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

The main business questions addressed in this project are:

- Who are the most valuable customers?
- Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
- How does customer behavior vary across segments?
- Which product categories drive revenue?
- What payment methods are most used?
- Does delivery performance impact customer satisfaction?
- How can customer retention strategies be improved?


---

# 📂 Dataset

## Source

Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist

The dataset contains real-world e-commerce transactions from a Brazilian marketplace.

It includes multiple relational tables:

| Dataset | Description |
|---|---|
| customers | Customer information and location |
| orders | Order lifecycle information |
| order_items | Products included in each order |
| payments | Payment methods and values |
| products | Product information |
| reviews | Customer satisfaction scores |
| sellers | Seller information |
| geolocation | Brazilian geographic data |
| category_translation | Product category translation |


Dataset size:

- ~100K customers
- ~100K orders
- ~112K order items
- ~103K payment records
- ~32K products

# 👥 Customer Segmentation — RFM Analysis

A customer segmentation analysis was performed using the RFM methodology.


## RFM Metrics

### Recency

Measures how recently a customer purchased.


### Frequency

Measures how often a customer purchased.


### Monetary

Measures the total revenue generated by a customer.


---

## Customer Segments

Customers were classified into four segments:


### 🏆 Champions

High-value customers with strong engagement.


### ⭐ Loyal Customers

Frequent customers generating significant revenue.


### ⚠️ At Risk

Customers with lower recent activity who may churn.


### 👤 Regular

Customers with normal purchasing behavior.


---

# 📊 Business Analysis


## Customer Segment Analysis

Key findings:

- Loyal Customers generate the highest total revenue contribution.
- Champions have the highest average customer value.
- High revenue is not always linked to purchase frequency.
- Some customers generate significant revenue through single high-value purchases.


---

## Revenue Analysis

Performed analysis on:

- Monthly revenue trends
- Revenue distribution by category
- Revenue contribution by customer segment


Key insight:

> Revenue is concentrated among specific customer groups and product categories, highlighting the importance of targeted strategies.


---

## Product Category Analysis

Analyzed:

- Top revenue-generating categories
- Category performance
- Customer satisfaction by category


Key insight:

> A limited number of categories contribute significantly to overall revenue.


---

## Customer Experience Analysis

Analyzed:

- Review scores
- Delivery performance
- Payment behavior


Key insight:

> Operational performance, especially delivery experience, plays an important role in customer satisfaction.

# 💡 Key Business Recommendations


## Customer Retention

- Target At Risk customers with retention campaigns.
- Encourage Regular customers to move toward Loyal segments.


## Loyalty Strategy

- Maintain Champions customers through exclusive rewards.
- Develop personalized offers for high-value customers.


## Revenue Optimization

- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing categories.
- Analyze customer purchasing patterns to increase basket value.


## Customer Experience

- Improve delivery processes to increase customer satisfaction.
